# E-commerce Sales Analytics & Customer Value Segmentation

## Project Overview

This project analyses transactional e-commerce sales data to uncover revenue trends, customer purchasing behaviour, and product performance.

The objective is to generate actionable business insights that support revenue growth and customer retention.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day.

Key questions include:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which countries contribute most sales?
- How does revenue change over time?
- Who are the most valuable customers?

---

## Dataset

Online Retail Dataset

Records:

Cleaned Dataset:

- 397,884 valid records

Period:
